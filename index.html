Design

A design is a plan or specification for the construction of an object or system or for the implementation of an activity or process, or the result of that plan or specification in the form of a prototype, product or process.

The verb to design expresses the process of developing a design. In some case, the direct construction fo an object without an explicit prior plan (such as in craftwork, some engineering, coding, an graphic design) may also be considered to be a design activity.
